About Us

Welcome to Inter Globe Technology Solutions. We are a leading provider of Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) services, dedicated to helping businesses achieve operational efficiency and growth.

Our Services

  • Inbound and Outbound Call Center Services

    Our call center services offer efficient handling of both inbound and outbound calls. We ensure exceptional customer service, telemarketing, lead generation, and more.

  • Customer Support and Help Desk

    We provide comprehensive customer support solutions, including help desk services, ticket management, troubleshooting, and customer relationship management (CRM).

  • Virtual Assistant Services

    Our virtual assistants are skilled professionals who can handle administrative tasks, scheduling, research, data entry, and other support services remotely.

  • Data Entry and Management

    We offer accurate and efficient data entry and management services, including data processing, data cleansing, database management, and data analysis.

  • Content Moderation and Data Annotation

    With our content moderation and data annotation services, we ensure the quality and accuracy of user-generated content, image recognition, sentiment analysis, and more.

  • Back Office Support

    Our back office support services cover various administrative tasks, document management, invoice processing, HR support, and other operational functions.

  • Technical Support

    We provide technical support services to assist customers with software, hardware, and IT-related issues through different channels, including phone, chat, and email.

  • E-commerce Support

    Our e-commerce support services help businesses manage online stores, order processing, inventory management, product listing, customer inquiries, and more.

  • Quality Assurance and Testing

    We offer comprehensive quality assurance and testing services to ensure the reliability, functionality, and usability of software applications and digital products.
